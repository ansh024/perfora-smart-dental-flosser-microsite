Frequently asked questions

A dental water flosser is an advanced high-tech version of the traditional string dental flosser. The device works automatically by shooting out pressurized water into the dental cavity — effectively targeting the areas between the teeth. This type of floss requires exponentially lesser manual effort and its benefits outrun those of all the various other oral care products. For instance, even with more time spent on the process the traditional strings are much less efficient than the electric devices.

If you are using the water flosser for the first time, we recommend using it on the soft mode. The stream of water is set to ensure there is no harm to the gums. You may face bleeding gums initially if you have not taken a dental treatment for a while. A step by step guide on how to use the flosser comes written in our product manual.

You may mix mouthwash, just use mouthwash or any dentist recommended solution to the water.

We recommend flossing once daily before bed at night. You may also consult your dentist for personalised recommendation on the same.

Our Smart Dental Flosser comes with an optimum tank capacity of 300ml which will last you enough water for flossing.

The Smart Dental Flosser has 5 modes for flossing: Soft — for beginners who are just getting started. Normal — for everyday flossing, once you know the drill. Pause — to avoid hurting sensitive areas. Neo Peo — 7 water pressure levels for a pro cleaning experience. DIY — to increase the water pressure for hard-to-reach areas.

The flosser comes with 5 nozzles with different identification to ensure multiple people can use the device. In addition, it has a 360° rotating nozzle that is ergonomically designed for efficient movement and deep cleaning.

The Smart Dental Flosser is rechargeable and comes with a C-type charging cable. One charge lasts minimum 30–45 days.
